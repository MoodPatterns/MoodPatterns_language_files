Scales and the time of the day

This view helps you to explore how your feelings are related to the time of the day.

The estimate tells you how you feel on average over the course of the day. The confidence range gives you insight how much your recorded values vary. If it is narrow, then most entries have been quite similar. A wide confidence range, on the other hand, indicates that you have entered high and low values at this time of the day.

If you do not want the day to start and end at midnight, then you can shift the starting time in the top bar at Adjust start time ( ⇄ ).

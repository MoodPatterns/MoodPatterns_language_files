Weekly report

The weekly report summarizes all entries of a whole week (Monday⁠–⁠Sunday) according to the currently selected Mood Scales and Additional Data. Where applicable the change from the previous week is shown.

The visible elements can be adjusted by clicking the filter-icon in the top control bar.

To share the report as a PDF click the share-icon in the top control bar.

Abbreviations & descriptions

abbr. desc.
val value: Indicates the average value and is calculated as the arithmetic mean.
spr spread: Indicates how far values are apart from one another. Calculated as the standard deviation.
dyn dynamic: Indicates how much values change from one entry to the next. Calculated as the mean absolute distance between consecutive values.
% percent: Indicates the proportion of the value in all non-missing values.
OR odds ratio: Indicates the relative increase of the item in relation to the total number of entries in the previous four weeks. E.g., 3 means that there are three times more entries than would be expected based on the item frequency in the month before.
